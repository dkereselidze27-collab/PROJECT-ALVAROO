Get to know us

At Alvaro, we believe a great haircut is more than just grooming — it’s a ritual of confidence. Our barbershop blends timeless techniques with modern edge, creating a space where every client feels welcome, relaxed, and sharp.

From classic cuts to tailored beard trims, we focus on precision, quality, and experience.Each service is delivered with attention to detail and the kind of care that’s rare to find.

Whether you’re coming in for your regular clean-up or a full transformation, we’re here to make you look and feel your best — every time.

Founder Story

Johny here — and welcome to my chair.

I started Alvaro with a pair of clippers, a passion for people, and the belief that every man deserves to look and feel his best. After years working in high-end barbershops, I decided to create a space that blends precision, personality, and genuine care. No gimmicks — just solid cuts, honest conversation, and a commitment to quality.

What began as a one-chair shop has grown into a small team of barbers who share the same values: respect the craft, know your tools, and treat every client like a regular.
