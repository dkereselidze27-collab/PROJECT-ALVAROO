FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What makes Alvaro different?

Alvaro blends classic barbering with modern style. It’s all about clean cuts, quality service, and a space that feels both laid-back and refined.

Do I need to book in advance?

While we accept walk-ins when available, we highly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time and barber. You can book easily through our online form.

What should I expect during my visit?

From the moment you arrive, you’ll be welcomed into a clean, comfortable space. Our team will consult with you to understand your style and provide a tailored experience.

Can I get a full grooming package?

Absolutely — we offer packages that include a haircut, beard trim, hot towel treatment, and more. Please check out our services page for full details and pricing.

Do you sell gift cards or memberships?

Yes, we offer digital gift cards, as well as grooming memberships with exclusive perks.

What products do you use?

We work with high-quality, barber-grade products that are carefully selected for performance and comfort. Feel free to ask your barber for personalized product tips!
